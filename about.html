Vision, Mission & Objectives

Building a new society based on cooperative principles and values.

Act as a manager, advisor, protector, agent, publicist, banker, investor, trainer, etc. to provide the needed assistance for the purpose of empowering the member entrepreneurs living in the Western Province of Sri Lanka according to the philosophy of the Society and the cooperative policies and cooperative laws, subject to the terms of the by-laws.

  • Development of saving, thrift, investment promotion in the membership.
  • Receiving and Aggregating Member and Non-Member Savings Deposits.
  • Introducing and implementing an effective credit system to rescue entrepreneurs, members, individuals and people who are stuck in a vicious cycle of debt through various methods.
  • Coordinating products, distribution sales and target market.
  • Contribute to development by identifying, unifying and utilizing existing resources in the jurisdiction.
  • Jewelery pawning services to assist in emergency situations.
  • Provision of agency services.
  • Contributing to investing resources in national development.
  • Providing education and training for business development.
  • Raising funds and investing effectively and safely.
  • Facilities for the payment of essential bills such as water, electricity and telephone for the ease of the customer.
  • Implementation of safeguards.
