General operations

How the cooperative runs day to day.

What is the Business Development Co-operative Society?
The Business Development Co-operative Society Limited was initiated in 2002 in the historical city of Mulleriyawa to serve through cooperative principles and to promote those who possess entrepreneurship characteristics & believe effort is an asset.

(Registered under the Cooperative Societies Act No. 05 of 1972 and Cooperative Societies Convention No. 03 of Western Provincial Council of 1998.
Registration number - WP/COOP/HO/05, Date of Registration - 2002-03-01.)
What core services do we provide?
A range of favourable savings and fixed deposits have been introduced for both members and other citizens. Also, a range of loan facilities have been introduced for developing existing businesses and for other purposes including education and pilgrimage.
  • Member and non-member deposits
  • Children's Savings
  • Fixed deposits
  • Loans to suit the needs of the members
  • Dialog eZ Cash, mCash
  • Telephone, water, electricity bill payments
  • Jewelery pawning service
  • Filling Station
  • Pharmacy
  • Navaloka Laboratory Service
  • Ceylinko Insurance, Coop Insurance, Third-party and Full Vehicle Insurance
  • Personal, Business, Travel, Property Insurance
What are the branch lobby hours?
Branches are open from 8:00 a.m. to 3:00 p.m. on business days.
Please schedule appointments for specialised desks so we can reduce wait times.

Digital Banking

Portal, app, and WhatsApp support.

Can I register without visiting a branch?
Yes. Use the online registration form with biometric selfie capture. OTP completes verification.
Is the portal accessible?
WCAG AA compliant with adjustable font sizes, high-contrast mode, and Sinhala/Tamil typography support.
Is WhatsApp support really 24/7?
Yes. WhatsApp agents operate in shifts to cover all time zones with escalation to human officers.

Other member services

Post office, pharmacy, and fuel counters.

What services are available at the cooperative post office?
You can send general or registered mail, purchase stamps, and settle small logistics tasks without leaving the banking complex.
What should I expect at the Salmal Uyana pharmacy?
  • Efficient service through WhatsApp.
  • Convenience of card payments.
  • Special discounts on the purchase of medicines.
  • Discount of 2.5% for bills above Rs. 2,000/-, and a discount of 5% for bills above Rs. 5,000/- for registered customers.
  • Channeling specialist doctors through Doc990.
  • Navaloka Laboratory Service.
  • Blood sugar testing at affordable rates.
  • Free blood pressure, height and weight (BMI) screening.
Is it possible to order medicine from the pharmacy by phone call / fax / email / WhatsApp?
Yes. Send your prescription via phone or WhatsApp to the pharmacy team for confirmation, payment, and further details.
