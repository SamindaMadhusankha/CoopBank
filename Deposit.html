Children's savings for every Sri Lankan below 18 and their guardians.

  • Open the account with a certified copy of the birth certificate.
  • Unlock an additional 10% annual interest or exercise books once the balance reaches LKR 5,000 (capped at LKR 3,000).

Dedicated to the courageous women of the nation with mentorship and safety nets.

  • Designed for entrepreneurs, homemakers, and collectives to build resilient savings.

Savings Deposits

Everyday savings with transparent rates, SMS alerts, and portal visibility.

Fixed Deposits

Lock in earnings for 03, 06, 09, or 12 months as a normal or automatic rollover deposit.

  • Eligible for special recognition at cooperative milestone events.
