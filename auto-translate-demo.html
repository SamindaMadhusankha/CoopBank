About Our Cooperative

Capital Cooperative Bank has been serving Sri Lankan communities for over 60 years. We are regulated by the Department of Cooperative Development and committed to ethical banking practices.

Our mission is to provide accessible financial services to all Sri Lankans, regardless of their economic background. We believe in transparency, community empowerment, and sustainable growth.

With over 80 branches across the island and more than 1.2 million satisfied members, we continue to grow and serve our communities with dedication and integrity.
